Simple Sugar Cookies

Description

Deliciously simple sugar cookies. Only takes a few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Flower
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • (Optional) Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix ingredients into large bowl.
  3. (Optional) Add vanilla extract.
  4. Shape mixed ingredients into 1 inch relatively flat.
  5. Place on baking sheet, using parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 25 mins or until fully cooked.
