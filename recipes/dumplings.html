Dumplings

Description

Yummy korean style dumplings. A easy to freeze for later recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ground pork
  • Ground beef
  • Green onions
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame sauce
  • Dumpling wrappers

Steps

  1. Mince green onions and garlic.
  2. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
  3. Add sauces.
  4. Place mixed ingredients in dumpling wrappers and fold into half circle.
  5. Freeze or boil in water for 8 mins.
